ISO 27001:2022 Certification Journey

End-to-End ISMS Implementation

As the lead, I managed the entire implementation of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) across all organizational units, resulting in **successful certification with zero major non-conformities.**

Phase 1: Initiation & Scoping

Duration: 3 Months

Goal: Establish the Foundation and Context of Security

  • **Scope Definition:** Formalized the ISMS scope, clearly identifying in-scope assets, processes, and systems (e.g., cloud infrastructure, data centers, core applications).
  • **Risk Framework:** Developed the organization's comprehensive risk assessment methodology, aligned with the ISO 27005 standard, and trained the management team on its application.
  • **Policy Baseline:** Authored and secured approval for core security documentation, including the high-level Information Security Policy and Statement of Applicability (SoA).
  • **Legal & Regulatory:** Mapped the company's activities to required legal, statutory, and contractual obligations (e.g., GDPR, local privacy laws) ensuring compliance was built into the ISMS.

Phase 2: Implementation & Operation

Duration: 6 Months

Goal: Deploy Technical Controls and Operationalize Security

  • **Control Deployment:** Directed IT and Operations teams in the implementation of **100+ required controls**, focusing on Annex A sections: Access Control, Cryptography, and Operations Security.
  • **Training Program:** Designed and deployed mandatory annual security awareness training for all staff, achieving a 99% completion rate within the required timeline.
  • **Continuous Monitoring:** Integrated security monitoring systems (SIEM) to automate event logging and alert generation, reducing manual detection time by 40%.
  • **Incident Response:** Established a tested Incident Response Plan (IRP), conducting tabletop exercises with key stakeholders to ensure operational readiness.

Phase 3: Audit & Certification

Duration: 2 Months

Goal: Validate Compliance and Achieve Certification

  • **Internal Audits:** Managed the internal audit program, overseeing corrective actions (CAPs) for all identified non-conformities before the external audit.
  • **Management Review:** Developed the official Management Review Meeting presentation, articulating ISMS performance against objectives and recommending continuous improvement initiatives.
  • **External Audit Liaison:** Served as the sole point of contact for the external certification body during both the Stage 1 (Documentation Review) and Stage 2 (Compliance Testing) audits.
  • **Result:** Successfully led the company to achieve official ISO 27001:2022 certification, reinforcing security credibility with clients and partners.
